Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add a transaction?
Tap the "+" button on the bottom navigation bar or the home screen. Enter the amount, select a category, and tap "Save". You can also use Quick Capture to scan a receipt.
How does the AI assistant work?
Simply chat with the AI in the "AI" tab. You can ask questions like "How much did I spend on dining this month?" or "Add a transaction for $20 coffee". The AI understands natural language.
How do I set a budget?
Go to the "Spending" tab and look for the "Budgets" section. Tap "Create Budget" or "Edit Categories" to set monthly limits for your spending.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your data is encrypted on your device and when synced to the cloud. We do not sell your data to third parties. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Monify AI to manage or cancel your subscription.
How do I delete my account?
In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your data from our servers.
