Monify AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Monify AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, budgets, goals, and other financial information you enter
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture, and preferences
  • Investment Data: Portfolio holdings and watchlist items you add

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app (only if analytics opted-in)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data when the app experiences errors

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • We do NOT access your bank accounts or financial institutions directly
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your financial data for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Monify AI service
  • Sync your data across devices (if you create an account)
  • Generate AI-powered insights about your finances
  • Send you notifications about budgets, bills, and goals (if enabled)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored encrypted on your device
  • Cloud Sync: If you create an account, data is synced to secure Firebase servers
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Authentication: Face ID/Touch ID available for additional security

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Shared Groups: When you create or join shared expense groups, group members can see shared transactions
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, and Qonversion for subscription management
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Generate financial reports in PDF format
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data at any time (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in Settings

7. Children's Privacy

Monify AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Firebase: Authentication and cloud storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Qonversion: Subscription management (Privacy Policy)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
